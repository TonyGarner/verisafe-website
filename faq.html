Frequently Asked Questions

What is VERISAFE and who is it for?

VERISAFE is an Australian-made cloud platform that lets organisations working with children and vulnerable people securely store, monitor, and stay on top of WWCC records. It's built for schools, childcare centres, sports clubs, churches, not-for-profits, and any business that needs to keep their team compliant under the Worker Screening Act 2020 (Vic) and similar laws across Australia. Think of it as your automated compliance co-pilot - monthly government checks (for supported states and territories), instant alerts, and a super-simple dashboard so you can focus on your work instead of chasing paperwork.

How does VERISAFE work?

You upload or add WWCC details for your team (easy bulk upload from spreadsheets). VERISAFE then automatically checks your records against the relevant government platforms monthly for currently supported states and territories. We are actively expanding both the frequency of checks and the number of jurisdictions covered. If anything changes - expiry coming up, revocation, or status update - you and your nominated admins get smart email notifications straight away. Everything lives in one secure Australian dashboard with team grouping so you can see compliance at a glance or drill down by department.

Which states and territories does VERISAFE support?

VERISAFE works across all Australian states and territories for storage and management. Automated monthly checks are currently available for selected states and territories, with more being added regularly as we expand. It's particularly strong in Victoria, but organisations operating nationally love the single-platform simplicity.

How secure is VERISAFE and what about privacy?

Extremely. All data is hosted in Australia, encrypted in transit and at rest, and protected by industry-standard security (including multi-factor authentication). We only use the information for the purpose you authorise - compliance monitoring and notifications - and we never sell or share it. VERISAFE is designed to help you meet the Australian Privacy Principles and Victorian privacy laws. You control access, and we have strict data-processing agreements in place.

What does it cost?

We keep it fair and predictable with team purchase packs (e.g. VERIFY25 lets you manage up to 25 WWCC records). We also offer VERI75, VERI200, VERI500 and VERI ENTERPRISE. No per-user monthly fees that balloon as your team grows - you buy the pack that suits your organisation size and you simply add/remove to evolve with your organsation. If you exceed your cap, simply upgrade and keep on going!

How do I get my existing team on board?

Super easy. You can bulk upload your current records via spreadsheet in minutes. New team members simply add their WWCC details or you can invite them to upload directly. Once added, VERISAFE takes over the rest. No more manual expiry tracking or chasing when its too late. Expiry alerts start from 90 days before expiry.

What notifications will I get?

You'll receive timely email alerts when: - A WWCC is due to expire soon - A WWCC has expired or been revoked (revoked status State/Territory dependent) - A new status update appears on the government system (State/Territory dependent) Admins can choose who gets notified and how often, so nothing slips through the cracks.

Can I manage volunteers, contractors, and casual staff too?

Absolutely. VERISAFE treats volunteers, contractors, and paid staff the same way - one dashboard.
