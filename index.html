VERISAFE offers organisations an easy and cost-effective solution for verified compliance and ongoing monitoring.

VERISAFE brings automation, timely notifications and confidence to ensure your records, employees and volunteers are compliant.

How does VERISAFE work?

VERISAFE is an easy to use, uncomplicated and reliable cloud platform, making it easy to log, manage WWCC records organisation wide.


Easy Management

Easily onboard new team members with VERISAFE. Simply add a new user, complete the key records and submit for securing filing. Review the organisation for a quick overview of compliance or line-by-line. Need to quickly transfer your current old records instantly to VERISAFE? We can support with bulk data uploads to save the manual data entry.

Regular Checks

VERISAFE checks with Victorian Government authorities each WWCC record to ensure compliance. Should a record expire or be revoked, VERISAFE will provide notification.

Team Purchase Packs

Keeping it simple, VERISAFE provides cost effective bundle packs allowing you to simply add as many team members to VERISAFE account. For example, purchase a VERI25 pack and you can add as many team members up-to 25x WWCC records. This ensure consistency in your monthly or annual billing. No surprises. View pack options here.

New Team Member Onboarding

Once a new volunteer completes their onboarding forms, an administrator sends them a unique secure link. The volunteer can then upload their Working With Children Check (WWCC) details themselves, allowing administrators to review and verify the details quickly and efficiently.
