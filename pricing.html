Pricing

Simple, transparent bundles designed to keep your compliance costs predictable.

VERISAFE is built around safety and practicality.

We know staff and volunteers come and go, and per-user pricing can quickly become expensive and unpredictable. That's why we created simple, fixed-price bundles.

One transparent price gives you the flexibility to add or remove as many people as you need, all within your bundle limit. If your requirements fall outside our standard bundles, email hello@verisafe.com.au and we'll find a solution that works for you.

Included in Every Plan

Regardless of the bundle size you choose, your organisation receives complete, unrestricted access to our core compliance tracking and record management platform.

Centralised Tracking

Ditch the messy, fragmented spreadsheets. VERISAFE provides a structured digital registry to securely log, categorise, and monitor your team's compliance data. Our interface ensures your documentation is organized perfectly, making internal reviews incredibly straightforward.

Proactive Expiry Alerts

Never let a critical renewal date slip through the cracks. Once your records are logged in our system, the platform tracks the calendar for you. Administrators can fully customise the notification schedule, setting tailored email countdown alerts at whatever specific day intervals best suit your team's unique operational workflows.

Status Change Oversight

Keep your leadership team informed whenever working with children clearances shift. VERISAFE makes it simple to flag, track, and manage critical updates across your active roster, ensuring your administration maintains the visibility required to protect your group.
