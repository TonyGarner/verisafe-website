Our Mission

To provide organisations an easy and cost-effective solution for verified Working with Children Check compliance and ongoing monitoring.

The VERISAFE Story

At VERISAFE, we're an Australian platform designed for organisations working with children and vulnerable people. We make it simple to store Working with Children's Checks (WWCC) for every state and territory, with daily automated checks against government systems to keep everything up to date and fully compliant. If a WWCC lapses or needs attention, the user or administrator is notified straight away - no guesswork, no last-minute panic. We don't just supply tools; we've created a trusted, secure environment where safety and compliance are built into everything we do.

By blending technical precision with a safeguarding-first approach, we've developed a platform that grows with your organisation and gives you complete peace of mind.

Our goal was to create a platform that not only automates the verification process but also provides timely notifications and a clear overview of compliance status. We believe that by making compliance easy, we can help organisations focus on what they do best, while ensuring the safety of the children in their care.
